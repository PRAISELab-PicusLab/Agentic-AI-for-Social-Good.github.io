Track Organizers

VM

Vincenzo Moscato, PhD

Full Professor, University of Naples Federico II
vincenzo.moscato@unina.it

Full Professor at the Electrical Engineering and Information Technology Department of University of Naples Federico II, responsible for teaching Database Systems and Big Data Engineering. Coordinator of the Master's Degree Program in Data Science and leader of the PICUS research group. His research focuses on Big Multimedia Data Analytics, Artificial Intelligence and Social Network Analysis. Director of CINI ITEM Research Lab, with numerous awards including grants from Oracle and Microsoft. Author of about 260 publications in international venues.

VL

Valerio La Gatta, PhD

Postdoctoral Fellow, Northwestern University
valerio.lagatta@northwestern.edu

Postdoctoral Fellow in Computer Science at Northwestern University, holding a PhD from University of Naples Federico II. His research focuses on agentic and generative AI systems for socially relevant domains, including social media, public information, and security. He studies the opportunities and risks of deploying autonomous AI systems in socially consequential domains, with a focus on impact, accountability, and alignment with social good.

GO

Gian Marco Orlando

PhD Student, University of Naples Federico II
gianmarco.orlando@unina.it

PhD Student at the University of Naples Federico II. He earned his Master's Degree in Computer Engineering with honors. His thesis highlights his expertise in the intersection of Artificial Intelligence and Social Network Analysis. His research interests lie in Social Network Analysis, Agent-Based Modeling, and Big Data Analytics.

DR

Diego Russo

PhD Student, University of Bergamo
diego.russo@unibg.it

PhD Student at the University of Bergamo. He earned his Master's Degree in Computer Engineering from the University of Naples Federico II. His thesis highlights his expertise in the challenges of Recommender Systems. His research interests lie in Recommender Systems, Social Network Analysis, and Big Data Analytics.
